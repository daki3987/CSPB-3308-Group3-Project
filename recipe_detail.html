Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy and delicious Italian pasta dish made with eggs, pancetta, and cheese. Simple ingredients, big flavor.

Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh ground black pepper
Instructions
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and immediately mix with pancetta and egg-cheese mix.
  5. Stir well, season with pepper, and serve hot.
